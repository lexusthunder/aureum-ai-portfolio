Privacy Policy

Aureum CRM · Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Aureum CRM ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, and role
  • Business Data: Lead information, property listings, and deal data you enter
  • Communications: Messages and notes within the app
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app features

We do NOT collect:

  • Financial information or payment details
  • Location data (unless explicitly granted for property features)
  • Contacts from your phone without permission

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Aureum CRM service
  • To power AI features including property matching and lead scoring
  • To send you relevant notifications about your CRM activity
  • To improve and personalize your experience
  • To provide customer support

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption (AES-256). We use Supabase for database hosting, which is SOC 2 Type 2 certified and GDPR compliant. Data is stored in EU-based servers by default.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. AI Processing

Aureum CRM uses Anthropic's Claude AI to power features such as property matching, lead scoring, and content generation. When you use these features, relevant data (lead preferences, property details) is processed by the AI model to generate recommendations. We do not use your data to train AI models.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Supabase (database), Anthropic (AI features) — under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights

Your client data is never shared with other Aureum users or third parties without your explicit consent.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to or restrict processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@aureum.ai

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Business data (leads, deals, properties) can be exported before deletion.

9. Children's Privacy

Aureum CRM is a B2B professional tool not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
