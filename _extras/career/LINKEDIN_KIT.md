## 5. Engagement strategy (30 min/day)

### Morning (10 min)

- Open LinkedIn feed
- Comment on 3 posts from your target list (10 ICs/EMs at Google)
- Substantive comments only — quote a specific point, add a counter or extension
- Why: you become familiar in their feed before any DM

### Afternoon (15 min)

- Reply to all DMs and comments on your posts
- Send 1-2 cold connection requests with personalized message
- Update your tracker spreadsheet

### Evening (5 min)

- Quick scan of your post performance
- Save 1-2 ideas to "future post" list
- Bookmark interesting posts to comment on tomorrow

## 8. What NOT to do on LinkedIn

| Don't | Why |
|---|---|
| Engagement bait ("agree?") | Killed in 2024, Linkedin algorithm penalises now |
| Job-hunt despair posts | Future employers see them |
| 100% self-promotion | Algorithm down-ranks |
| Polls (unless very specific) | Low signal |
| Reposting other people's content | Almost no reach in 2026 |
| AI-generated obvious content | Detected, penalised |
